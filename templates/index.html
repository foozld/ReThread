About Us

The core of the Re-Thread is to help users understand the environmental impact and sustainability of different clothing materials, but also to save money. The user will be able to input or search for specific fabric, such as polyester, and the site will return clear information about its enviromental effects.

The results would include factors such as water usage, carbon footprint, microplastic pollution, and whether the material is biodegradable. The site will provide a simple sustainability rating to help users quickly judge how environmentally friendly the material is. In addition, Re-Thread would highlight key issues related to the fabric, such as chemical processing or pollution, and suggest more sustainable alternatives. By presenting this information in a simple and accessible way, the website aims to educate consumers and encourage more sustainable choices when purchasing clothing.
